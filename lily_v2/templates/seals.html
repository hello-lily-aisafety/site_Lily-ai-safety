LILY Safety Seals

Our certification framework recognizes AI companies committed to safety, gender equity, and the protection of women and children. Click each seal to learn more.

Requirements

Public declaration of zero-tolerance for misogyny in Terms of Use; Commitment to 1 internal training session per year.

◈ Each certified company receives a unique QR Code linking to their validation page on the LILY website.

Requirements

All Bronze requirements + Active human supervision reports; Transparency on data filtering methods; Active Listening Channel for LILY reports.

◈ Each certified company receives a unique QR Code linking to their validation page on the LILY website.

Requirements

All Silver requirements + Mandatory Red Teaming focused on gender bias; Support for external workshops; Direct API access for LILY technical audits.

◈ Each certified company receives a unique QR Code linking to their validation page on the LILY website.

Core Eligibility

All applicants must have an absolute zero-tolerance policy for content promoting dehumanizing narratives against women and girls, as defined by International Human Rights and Equity standards.

Strategic Pillars

  • Human-in-the-Loop: Verifiable human oversight to mitigate hate speech.
  • Data Literacy Commitment: Internal workshops on identifying subtle misogyny in language models.
  • Active Feedback Loop: Commitment to reviewing and rectifying failures reported by LILY.
  • QR Code Verification: Every seal features a unique QR Code linking to a validation page.
