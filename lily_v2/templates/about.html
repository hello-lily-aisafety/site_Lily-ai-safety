Who We Are

About LILY

Our Mission

LILY AI Safety Alliance — Lead Initiative for Liberty & Youth in AI — was born from the conviction that the development of artificial intelligence must be accompanied by serious research on safety, alignment, and social impact.

Our mission is to mitigate systemic harms and algorithmic violence that disproportionately affect women and children. We operate at the frontier of AI Safety, developing governance frameworks, mandatory technical standards, and audit protocols to ensure that technological development is inseparable from the protection of human rights and digital dignity.

Our Vision

To become the leading global technical and advisory authority on AI governance — promoting a human-centered digital ecosystem where gender safety and the protection of minors are non-negotiable, interoperable global infrastructures.

"If we focus on giving voice to the safety of women and children in the digital environment, in the future the world will no longer need our institute — because everyone will know their ethical responsibilities in the safety and preservation of humanity."

Core Values

◈

Digital Gender Equity

Eradication of misogynistic bias in language models and generative systems.

◎

Safety by Design

Safety must be implemented in the training phase, not only as a reactive filter.

⬡

Accountability

Corporate and technical responsibility for the social impacts of emerging technologies.

❋

Inclusive Sovereignty

Security solutions must reflect the needs of global populations, respecting each country's autonomy.

⚡

The IA 50/50 Commitment

LILY is committed to advocating for at least 50% of women in technical, strategic, and leadership positions in AI. We believe equitable representation is not optional — it is a structural requirement for safe and beneficial AI.
